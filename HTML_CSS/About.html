What is HotLogBook

Does this day sound familier? You go to work, or your banks website, and attempt to login only to find that you cannot remember your password. UGH! Now you have to spend valuable time trying to remeber what your password is or, even worse, hit that dreaded forgot password button and waste your time answering ridiculous questions. HotLogBook was created to alleviate this problem by remembering your passwords for you.

Usage instructions

To use HotLogBook follow these steps:

1). If you are a new user click on the "create new account" page.

2). Login to your account.

3). To enter new account information hit the "add account" button.

4). Fill out the required information.

5). Click the submit button.

6). If you would like to view your stored accounts press the "view my logbook" button.
